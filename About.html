About us

Daze Office Enterprise is a Malawian-owned and operated company, established in June 2021 in the capital city, Lilongwe. The enterprise is led by a dedicated and highly skilled team with diverse expertise, committed to delivering reliable stationery and internet services, as well as general information and communication technology (ICT) solutions tailored for schools, offices, and communities across Malawi. In addition to ICT and office solutions, Daze Office Enterprise also offers beauty enhancement services and provides high-quality training in basic computer skills and music, empowering individuals with practical and creative knowledge for personal and professional development.
